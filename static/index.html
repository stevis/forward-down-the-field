Story

Any sports fan will tell you that the Detroit Lions are one of the most beleaguered franchises in the history of not only the NFL but of North American Sports in general. They hold nearly every embarrassing record in the NFL book, and have never won more than one playoff game let alone making a run at the Super Bowl. After more than 50 years of losing, a small group of Lions fans have finally had enough. Forward down the field is the quest of life long Lions fans to discover how their team became the butt of an NFL joke and what it will take to bring a Super Bowl to Detroit.

Along the way they will talk to former players, die hard fans and people in the Detroit sports community to get their take on what the team has meant to them over the years, how it has effected their lives and how the team is deeply sown into the fabric of South East Michigan. They will examine the parallels between the

Detroit and the team the represents it on gridiron. Both have fallen on hard times and are in desperate need of a comeback. For a never give up, blue collar town, thirsty for a win on the field and off, a victory could make all of the difference. For once that victory could be right around the corner.
