Story

Any sports fan will tell you that the Detroit Lions are one of the most beleaguered franchises in the history of not only the NFL but of North American Sports in general. They hold nearly every embarrassing record in the NFL book, and have never won more than one playoff game let alone making a run at the Super Bowl.

Forward Down the Field is the quest of a few life-long Lions fans to discover how their team became the butt of an NFL joke and what it will take to bring a Super Bowl to Detroit. Along the way they will talk to former players, die hard fans, and people in the Detroit sports community to get their take on what the team has meant to them over the years, how it has affected their lives, and how the team is deeply sewn into the fabric of Michigan. They will examine the parallels between the city of Detroit and the team that represents it on the gridiron. Both have fallen on hard times and are in desperate need of a comeback.

For a never give up, blue collar town that is ready for a win on the field and off, a victory could make all of the difference. For once that victory could be right around the corner. It is a journey of passion, betrayal, hope and pride where the audience will discover what it really means to be a Lions Fan.

Engage

Forward Down the Field aims to tell the Detroit Lions fans’ story. In order to do a just that, we need your (the fans) help. We are asking for home video footage, photographs or audio recordings of you, your family and friends, or anyone you know that has video of Detroit Lions fandom. Old home movies, tailgate traditions, playing catch in the backyard. ANYTHING! The more stories we can tell, the more complete the film becomes. Any format, any era, any story will help us greatly. Contact Mike Fortunato for more information on how you can help tell our story.

We will also be doing several social media campaigns throughout production to include the fans and their amazing stories. The more content the fans can generate, the richer the film becomes. Who knows? Maybe your story will become a part of film? Keep visiting our social media outlets for more information regarding these campaigns and for exclusive content. Your stories are a part of all of our story!
